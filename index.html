Creative Experiments

WishVault
UX Research Mobile App Figma
Problem

Indian online shoppers spread their saved items across Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra, Nykaa, Meesho and more. There is no single place to see everything, track prices, or share lists with friends and family.

Process

Conducted market research, analyzed existing wishlist and price-tracking tools, and mapped the shopping journey to identify friction points. Designed a seamless experience centered around one-click product saving, unified wishlists, and automatic price alerts.

Result

Created WishVault a universal wishlist platform that brings every saved product into one place, helping users track prices, organize purchases, and shop smarter.
